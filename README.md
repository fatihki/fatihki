## Hi there 👋

I am a statistician and researcher with a PhD in Mathematical Statistics. My research focuses on the development of statistical methodology in survival analysis and reliability theory. I am particularly interested in designing robust statistical methods and developing open-source software for applied researchers.

<br>

**Research Interests:**
Survival Analysis - Cure Rate Models - High-Dimensional Statistics - Machine Learning - Reliability Theory and Analysis - Statistical Reliability Modeling - Stress–Strength Reliability - Bayesian Inference 

<br>

**R packages:**

- Cure Rate Models: [penMCFM](https://github.com/fatihki/penMCFM), [BayesSMCM](https://github.com/fatihki/BayesSMCM)

- Reliability Analysis: [SSReliabilityClaytonMWD](https://cran.r-project.org/package=SSReliabilityClaytonMWD)
